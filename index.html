Land of the Snake

Instructions

Welcome to the land of the snake. Beware!

In the Land of the Snake, food is plentiful and the snake is king! Roam the wilds freely, eat, grow long and become fit and fast.

In Snake Land, you are free to roam where you please and the land has no boundries, however, you will always come back!!

One point to remember, however, the fitter and faster you get, the smaller your forest will become. Hit yourself, and you will be no more!
